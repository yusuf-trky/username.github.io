About me

I'm a third-year informatics student at the University of Oslo. What I enjoy most is taking something technical from a lecture and finding out where it actually lives in the real world — like learning routing algorithms, then realising they're the reason the internet works at all.

The highlight so far: building Sydvest, an Android weather app, with a team of five. We worked in weekly sprints with pull requests and code review — as close to a real engineering team as a course gets. Between that, coursework, and personal projects, I've gotten comfortable in Java, Kotlin, Python and C.
